About

Hello there! I'm Aulia, a Frontend/Fullstack-Jamstack web developer based in Jakarta, Indonesia. I wake up in the morning, do some pray and grab a cup of timmies coffee, open up my laptop and build things on the web. I work as a private class teacher as well in the afternoon.

Some have called me a dreamer, and they're not entirely wrong, considering my enthusiasm for unconventional interests in my neighborhood.

I recently created a YouTube channel called Growing Indonesia where I upload videos on frontend web development topics.
